Our Equipment

Bench Press

The barbell bench press is a classic exercise popular among all weight lifting circles. It primarily works your pectoralis major (chest) along with other muscles.

Variations:

  • Incline bench press
  • Decline bench press
  • Dumbbell press

Deadlift

The deadlift is a great way to build strong legs and butt. You lift the weight from the ground to thigh-level using primarily your leg and hip muscles.

Variations:

  • Sumo Deadlift
  • Conventional Deadlift

Squat Rack

The squat is a vital exercise for increasing the strength and size of the lower body muscles as well as developing core strength.

Variations:

  • Back Squat
  • Front Squat
  • Box Squat
